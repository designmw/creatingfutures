## Is your CV getting you interviews?

Are you:

- Seeking new employment, a promotion, or a career change?
- Applying for roles but not being invited to interview?
- Unsure how to structure your CV or present yourself on paper?
- Struggling to communicate your professional value effectively?

Only one person will get the job you're after — and you need an edge to be that person. With 25 years' experience creating professional, impactful and engaging CVs, I write achievement-led CVs with recruiters in mind, for senior professionals, career changers, graduates and those starting out.

## How it works

1. Send me your current CV and the job specifications for roles that interest you.
2. We meet online to discuss your experience and the role you're targeting.
3. I rewrite your CV in detail — targeted to the role and focused on your achievements.
4. We review the draft together on a follow-up call.
5. You receive your edits — the whole process typically takes 5–7 days.
6. You get final Word and PDF versions, so you can keep it updated yourself.
