## Does your cover letter give your CV an edge?

Are you:

- Unsure what a strong cover letter should contain?
- Finding it hard to align your skills with a specific role?
- Reusing the same generic cover letter for every application?

A well-written, targeted cover letter that doesn't duplicate your CV is worth the effort. It should position you as the ideal candidate for the role by highlighting your motivation for the position, how your skills match the job requirements, and how your experience and values match the needs of the recruiter.
