## Do you have an upcoming interview — or interviews that aren't converting to offers?

Do any of these sound familiar?

- You find it hard to answer questions confidently without rambling.
- It's been a long time since your last interview and you're anxious.
- You're unsure how competency-based or strengths-based interviews work.
- You're nervous about presenting yourself to a panel.
- The stakes are high — this is a role you really want.
- You're getting interviews, but no offers.

Over the last 25 years I have worked with hundreds of recruiters. I understand how they interview and what they're looking for from candidates. Working with me will ensure you are prepared to make a strong, positive impression at interview.

## How it works

1. **Preparation** — I analyse the job specification, your CV and the interview details to identify the questions you're likely to face.
2. **Initial consultation (30 minutes)** — we agree the main areas to focus on and I brief you on the questions to prepare.
3. **First mock interview** — you practise your answers and get detailed feedback on areas to improve.
4. **In-depth practice interview** — you apply the techniques, with comprehensive feedback.

By the end, you'll have numerous specific, tangible examples that demonstrate the competencies and strengths the recruiter is looking for. [Make an inquiry](/contact) to start preparing.
