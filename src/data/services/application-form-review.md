## Is your application form strong enough to secure an interview?

Are you:

- Unsure whether your answers address the criteria for the role?
- Concerned you're not showcasing your skills and experience effectively?

Application forms — particularly civil and public service competency-based forms — are assessed against specific criteria. A strong form demonstrates, with evidence, exactly what the assessors are looking for.

## How it works

1. Send me your completed application form along with the job description.
2. We meet online to discuss your experience and the role.
3. I review your form in detail and suggest improvements to your answers.
4. Your revisions are checked to ensure they align with the role and its competency requirements.
